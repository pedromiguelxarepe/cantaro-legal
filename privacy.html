Privacy Policy

Effective from the app's first public release

Privacy Policy Terms of Use

This Privacy Policy explains how the Cantaro app (the "App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. The App is built to respect your privacy: it works entirely on your device and does not require an account.

The short version

We do not collect, store, or share personal information about you. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. What you do in the App stays on your device.

Information stored on your device

To work properly, the App saves small pieces of information locally on your device using the system's standard storage. This includes your preferences (such as whether reversed cards are enabled), your selected deck, your card of the day, and a record of which in-app purchases you own.

This information never leaves your device and is not visible to us.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details. Apple may process information to complete your purchase in line with Apple's Privacy Policy. The App only learns whether a purchase completed, so it can unlock the matching content.

No analytics or advertising

The App does not currently include third-party analytics or advertising, and it does not use tracking technologies or share data with advertisers. If this ever changes, we will update this Policy and, where required, ask for your consent before any such feature is enabled.

Data retention and deletion

Because the App stores information only on your device, you stay in control of it. Deleting the App removes the information it stored on your device. Records of your purchases are kept by Apple and can be restored through the App Store.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. It contains themes related to tarot and divination and is intended for a general audience consistent with its App Store age rating.

Third-party services

The App relies on Apple's App Store and in-app purchase services. Your use of those services is governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The latest version is always available in the App, with the effective date shown above.
